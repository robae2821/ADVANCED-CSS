How To Tie A Tie

Js

Step 1

Drape tie around your neck, extending the wide end about 12 inches below narrow end. Then Cross wide end over narrow and Continue around.

Step 2

Pass wide end across front of narrow once more.Then Pass wide end up through loop. After that Holding front of knot loose with index finger. Pass wide end down through loop in front.

Step 3

Remove finger and tighten knot carefully. Draw up tight to collar by holding narrow end and sliding knot up snug.
